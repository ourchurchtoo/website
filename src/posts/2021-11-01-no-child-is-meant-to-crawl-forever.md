You have taught me how to walk,\
I stumble but you are near,\
Ready to catch,\
You have taught me how to run,\
I am slow to start but you are near,\
Ready to encourage,\
You have taught me how to fly,\
I can't see the floor beneath but you are near,\
Ready to soar,\
Together.
